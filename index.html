Type of Zubr

European Zubr

American Zubr

The European Zubr is a ruminant mammal of the Bovidae family. The average weight of the male is around 800 kg, and the size can reach 1.80 m. The animal can live 15 to 20 years. They live mainly in the large forest of Białowieża, in eastern Poland.

His brother, the American Zubr live in all of north america. They are biger than European, adult weight is around 500 to 600 kg for females and 800 to 1100 kg for males. The animal can live 25 to 30 years.

The main diferance betwen European and American Zubr are :

  • the body of European is more slender than that of the American bison
  • The limbs are longer
  • The head is carried higher
  • The horns extend beyond the cover of the head

in the 19th century there was other breed of bison in europe, north america and asia. But they have disappeared due to mass hunting.

Today, the bison are animals threatened with extinction and are therefore protected.
